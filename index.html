Privacy Policy & Downloads

Last updated: March 29, 2026 Desktop App

Screen Time Manager believes in complete digital privacy. No cloud syncing. No personal tracking. Just a healthy workflow tool that stays entirely on your local machine.

1. Zero Data Collection

Screen Time Manager is a truly local application. We never ask for, collect, or transmit your name, email, or usage patterns to any external servers. Your habits are your business.

✓ No sign-ups · No analytics tracking · No behavioral profiling

2. Local Data Storage

To function properly and provide you with daily usage tracking, the app stores data securely on your own device:

  • No Cloud Sync: We do not connect to third-party databases, meaning there is zero risk of your data being intercepted or sold.

You can easily delete all your tracking history at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the local application folder.

3. Children’s Privacy

Because our application operates completely offline and collects zero data, it is inherently safe for users of all ages, including children who are managing their study time or screen habits.

4. Policy Updates

If we ever introduce optional cloud-syncing features in the future, we will update this policy and notify users. Any such features will always be strictly opt-in.

5. Open Source & Contact

Want to verify our code? Have questions or feedback? You can view the source code and open an issue directly on GitHub.
